Executive Administrative Assistant to Founder and CEO

  1. (3 Month Contract to Full-time Hire, Remote)

Role:

We seek an executive assistant who can pick up activities after our CEO and drive his actions home. Must naturally enjoy task and people management. Words that describe you: empathetic, organized, and scrappy. This is definitely a position for someone who is not afraid of ambiguity.

You are perfect for this role if you:

Want to shape the future of an organization committed to connecting professionals offline in innovative ways. Digitally native, resourceful, and a confident problem-solver always looking to do things better and smarter.

If this sounds like you, keep reading.

As the Executive Administrative Assistant, you will serve as critical support to our Founder and CEO, providing administrative support in select day-to-day needs and long-term projects / initiatives. In this new role, you will have the opportunity to plan events for diverse audiences, lead a social media campaign, and influence our brand development.

Additionally, you will be responsible for tackling his personal as well as work issues by taking on a variety of tasks in order to help him focus on his primary job. This includes posting ads, booking travel, handling living arrangements, and even eBay-like buyer/selling.

Key Responsibilities:

  • • Move opportunities/projects forward regardless of obstacles or challenges
  • • Improve business operations
  • • Manage projects & events
  • • Interact with customers
  • • Manage recruiting
  • • Handle 3rd party vendor negotiations

Required Skills:

  • • Be driven to accomplish a lot and learn a LOT very quickly
  • • Ability to prioritize tasks and track progress
  • • Ability to work with outside contractors and other employees
  • • Able to effectively give and receive constructive feedback
  • • Must have a no-excuse, results-focused mindset

Minimum requirements:

  • • Prior experience as an EA, in Marketing, or Sales
  • • Skilled at Asana for project/task management

Other Applicable Job Titles:
  • Admin
  • Event Planner
  • Community Builder
