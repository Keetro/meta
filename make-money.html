How does this work?

Keetro.org is a platform that coordinates people with diverse range of skills to make interesting ideas happen. We think that the best way for this to work is to help people (we call them "idea sponsors") start professional events, in which there is enough exclusivity/intimacy among peers so that individuals can feel open about sharing their ambitions and expertise.

How exactly will I be helping?

We’ve built keetro.org with an idea thatanyone can be an event planner, marketer, or one-time community organizer. If you have passion for any of those activities and you are matched with one of our idea sponsors, then you will be expected to be an authority on one or more aspects of the job--be that catering, digital marketing, copy, design, etc..
